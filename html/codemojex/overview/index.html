Codemojex · chapter C0

Overview — the whole game, at a glance.

Codemojex is a real-time, multiplayer code-breaking competition that runs as a Telegram Mini App — Mastermind played with an emoji keyboard, for money. This chapter opens the course the way the design opens the system: the game placed in its family, the engine that treats every mode as policy on the same branded entities, and the four-layer architecture one glance can hold. It is the shipped game (cm.1–cm.7) taught as-built.

Pick a mode below. The four policy cells change; the scoring cell does not.
