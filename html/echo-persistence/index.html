Echo Persistence · 13 modules · 4 chapters

Durable state, taught as built.

EchoMQ enqueues on Valkey — fast and volatile. Echo Persistence is the floor beneath it: an in-heap accept tier, a transactional page-store built twice (Elixir on CubDB, Rust on Fjall), and replication to Tigris. The course builds that ladder from the bottom up, and every module is an interactive dive into a mechanism, not a table of machine numbers.
