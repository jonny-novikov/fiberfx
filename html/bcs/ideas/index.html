B1 · Ideas Behind — manuscript Part I

The conceptual floor.

Part I is the floor every later Part stands on: the law in three clauses, the identity contract read as architecture, the storage economics that priced the contract, the ECS arc that distribution completes, the time inside the name, and the encode trial that closes it.

The manuscript states Part I as assertion and argument; Part II begins demonstrating. Each module below teaches one written chapter, with its committed evidence quoted verbatim on the page.
