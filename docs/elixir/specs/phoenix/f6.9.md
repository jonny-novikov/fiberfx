# F6.9 · The live dashboard (capstone)

> Compose the whole chapter into one read-only page. `PortalWeb.DashboardLive` seeds metric counts from the F6.4
> contexts at mount, subscribes to the domain `"events"` topic, and folds each broadcast into its socket read model —
> bumping a count and prepending a capped activity-feed row — while a `Presence` count shows who is watching. The route
> lives under the F6.8 auth, is read-only, and is correct across a cluster. It adds no write path and no new domain
> event.

## Goal

After F6.9, the Portal has a read-only, real-time operations dashboard. `PortalWeb.DashboardLive` holds a read model on
its socket: `courses_count` from `Catalog.count_courses/0` and `enrollments_count` from `Enrollment.count/0`, seeded at
mount as assigns, plus a capped `stream(:events, …)` for an activity feed. On its connected mount it subscribes to the
domain `"events"` topic (the F6.7 `Portal` PubSub facade) and tracks itself in `"dashboard"` Presence. `handle_info/2`
folds each broadcast into the read model — `{:course_created, course}` bumps `courses_count` and prepends a feed row,
`{:enrolled, enrollment}` bumps `enrollments_count` and prepends a row, and `%{event: "presence_diff"}` recomputes
`viewers`. `render/1` draws HEEx metric cards from assigns and the feed under `phx-update="stream"`. The route lives in
a `live_session` under the F6.8 auth, read-only and correct across a cluster.

## Rationale (5W)

- **Why** — A platform earns an operations view: one page that folds the same events the system already broadcasts into
  live counts and a feed, so operators and instructors see activity as it happens without querying. It is the capstone
  because it composes everything below — contexts for the seed, PubSub for the stream, Presence for the count, streams
  for the feed, auth for access, clustering for correctness — into a single read-only LiveView with no write path.
- **What** — the dashboard LiveView with a socket read model, a subscription to the domain events topic, the
  `handle_info` fold, the HEEx render, a Presence viewer count, and the authed route.
- **Who** — Instructors (a live view of learning activity) and Operators (live operational metrics).
- **When** — the ninth and final Phoenix rung; it composes F6.4 (contexts), F6.6 (LiveView/streams), F6.7
  (PubSub/Presence), and F6.8 (auth/clustering) into one page; nothing comes after it in the chapter.
- **Where** — `lib/portal_web/live/dashboard_live.ex` (mount/render/handle_info), `lib/portal_web/router.ex` (a
  `live_session` under F6.8 auth), and the existing `Portal` PubSub facade plus `PortalWeb.Presence` from F6.7.

## Scope

- **In** — `PortalWeb.DashboardLive` holding a socket read model (counts as assigns seeded from the F6.4 contexts, a
  capped events stream); subscribing to the domain `"events"` topic on the connected mount; the `handle_info/2` fold
  (bump counts, prepend feed rows, recompute viewers); the HEEx render (metric cards + feed under
  `phx-update="stream"`); `Presence` `track/3` + `presence_diff` for a viewer count; the `count_courses/0` /
  `Enrollment.count/0` reads and the `{:enrolled, _}` broadcast (the F6.7 ok-only broadcast applied to enrollment) that
  the fold needs; and the route under F6.8 auth, read-only and cluster-correct.
- **Out** — any write or mutation from the dashboard (it is read-only); new domain features or new event types beyond
  folding existing broadcasts; historical or time-series analytics beyond the live counts and capped feed;
  export/reporting; and role-based views beyond authenticated access.

## Deliverables

- **F6.9-D1** — `PortalWeb.DashboardLive` with a socket read model: `courses_count` from `Catalog.count_courses/0` and
  `enrollments_count` from `Enrollment.count/0` seeded at mount (as assigns), plus a capped `stream(:events, …)` for
  the activity feed. (The two `count` functions are small additions to the F6.4 contexts.)
- **F6.9-D2** — a subscription on the connected mount (behind `connected?/1`): `Portal.subscribe("events")` for domain
  events and `Presence.track/3` in `"dashboard"`.
- **F6.9-D3** — the fold in `handle_info/2`: `{:course_created, course}` bumps `courses_count` and
  `stream_insert(:events, row, at: 0)`; `{:enrolled, enrollment}` bumps `enrollments_count` and prepends a row;
  `%{event: "presence_diff"}` recomputes `viewers` from `Presence.list("dashboard")`.
- **F6.9-D4** — the render: HEEx metric cards drawn from `@courses_count`, `@enrollments_count`, and `@viewers`, plus
  the feed rendered under `phx-update="stream"`; read-only, with no form or write control.
- **F6.9-D5** — a Presence viewer count: `viewers` is `map_size(Presence.list("dashboard"))`, tracked at mount and
  recomputed on `presence_diff`, correct across the cluster.
- **F6.9-D6** — the route: `live "/dashboard", DashboardLive` inside a `live_session` under the F6.8 auth (`on_mount`
  requiring an authenticated user), read-only.
- **F6.9-D7** — verification: the counts seed at mount; a `{:course_created, _}` and an `{:enrolled, _}` each bump the
  right count and prepend a feed row live; the viewer count tracks connect and disconnect across nodes; the page is
  read-only and requires auth; and the feed is a capped stream (no unbounded list).

## Invariants

- **F6.9-INV1 (read-only)** — the dashboard issues no command and no write; it only seeds and folds broadcasts; there
  is no form or mutating control on the page.
- **F6.9-INV2 (seed then fold)** — counts are seeded once from the F6.4 contexts at mount and thereafter updated only
  by folding broadcasts; the dashboard does not re-query on every event.
- **F6.9-INV3 (over the facade — the master invariant)** — the dashboard seeds via the contexts' public counts and
  subscribes via the `Portal` PubSub facade; it names no engine, repo, or `GenServer.call`, and renders only assigns
  and the stream.
- **F6.9-INV4 (capped feed via a stream)** — the activity feed is a `stream` (capped), so it is rows in the DOM with no
  unbounded list in socket memory.
- **F6.9-INV5 (authed and cluster-correct)** — the route is under the F6.8 auth (an anonymous user cannot reach it),
  and the viewer count and event fold are correct across the cluster (PubSub + Presence from F6.7/F6.8).
- **F6.9-INV6 (composition, no new domain)** — the capstone composes existing capabilities (contexts, PubSub, Presence,
  streams, auth) and folds existing events; it introduces no new domain event type or write path.

## Definition of Done

- [ ] `DashboardLive` seeds `courses_count`/`enrollments_count` from the F6.4 contexts at mount (F6.9-D1, INV2).
- [ ] It subscribes to `"events"` and tracks `"dashboard"` Presence on the connected mount (F6.9-D2).
- [ ] `handle_info/2` bumps the right count and prepends a feed row for `{:course_created, _}` and `{:enrolled, _}`,
      and recomputes `viewers` on `presence_diff` (F6.9-D3).
- [ ] The render shows metric cards from assigns and a feed under `phx-update="stream"`; the page is read-only
      (F6.9-INV1, INV4).
- [ ] The route is in a `live_session` under F6.8 auth; an anonymous user is redirected (F6.9-INV5).
- [ ] The dashboard names no engine, repo, or `GenServer.call`; it folds existing events only (F6.9-INV3, INV6).
- [ ] Every deliverable maps to a story and every invariant is checked (see `./f6.9.stories.md`).
