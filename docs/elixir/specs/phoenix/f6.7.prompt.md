## The rung in one paragraph

F6.7 pushes F6.6's per-socket catalog state across **every connected client**. The `Portal` facade gains a
PubSub wrapper (`subscribe/1`, `broadcast/2` over a supervised `Phoenix.PubSub`); `Portal.Catalog` broadcasts
`{:course_created, _}` / `{:course_updated, _}` **only after a successful write** (a `broadcast/2` helper that
fires on `{:ok, _}`); `CatalogLive` subscribes to `"courses"` on its **connected mount** (the exact
`connected?/1` seam F6.6 left as a no-op marker) and applies `stream_insert/3` in `handle_info/2` (prepend on
create, replace on update, keyed by id); `PortalWeb.Presence` `track/3` + a `presence_diff` handler yields a
cluster-correct viewer count. The only new supervision children are `Phoenix.PubSub` (in `Portal.Application`)
and `PortalWeb.Presence` (in `PortalWeb.Application`); everything below the facade is unchanged. Triad:
[`f6.7.md`](f6.7.md) · [`f6.7.stories.md`](f6.7.stories.md) · [`f6.7.llms.md`](f6.7.llms.md).

## Mode — Flat-L2 lead-team, review gates
